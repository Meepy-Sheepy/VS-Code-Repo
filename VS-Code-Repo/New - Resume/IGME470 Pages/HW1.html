First Light

To make my first circuit, all I needed was an LED, resistor, switch, and a couple of jumper cables. All in all, it was pretty simple and figuring out how Power and Ground work together and the path that is required in order to make the LED light up is starting to make some sense. LED's are polarized components, which means they only allow electricity to flow through them in one direction. There are 2 legs on the LED, an anode and cathode. Anode, the longer leg connects to Power while Cathode, the shorter leg, connects to Ground.
